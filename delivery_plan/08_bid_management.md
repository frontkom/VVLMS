## 5. Award Criteria Mapping

Understanding the scoring model is critical for bid strategy. Quality accounts for 80% and Price for 20%.

| Criterion | Weight | Max Points | Key Evidence Documents |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Quality (total)** | **80%** | **7.0** | |
| Functional requirements | 50% of quality | 3.5 | Bilag 1 vedlegg 1 response, Vedlegg 5 |
| Non-functional requirements | 20% of quality | 1.4 | Bilag 1 vedlegg 2 response, Bilag 2 section 2.3 |
| Usability (UX) | 12.5% of quality | 0.875 | Bilag 2 section 2.2, POC demos, hands-on testing |
| Establishment & implementation capability | 12.5% of quality | 0.875 | Bilag 3 |
| Business principles & standard terms | 5% of quality | 0.35 | Bilag 7 (minimal deviations) |
| **Price** | **20%** | **3.0** | Bilag 6, Prisskjema |

**Strategic implications:**
- Functional requirements are the dominant factor (50% of 80% = 40% of total score)
- UX/usability evaluation includes live demo and hands-on testing - invest heavily in demo preparation
- Price is only 20% - compete on quality, not price; lowest price gets 10 points, others scored linearly
- Business principles: minimize contract deviations to avoid losing points or being disqualified
- All A-krav must be "Yes" - a single "No" leads to rejection

**Selection criteria weighting:**
- Experience from comparable LMS engagements: **65%**
- Capacity to execute the contract: **35%**

### 8.2 Reference Strategy (65% weight)

References are the dominant qualification factor. Each reference must demonstrate:
- Cloud-based LMS/learning platform delivery
- Comparable organizational scale (thousands of users)
- Norwegian/Nordic public sector experience (strongly preferred)
- Integration complexity (SSO, HR systems, reporting)
- External user management (portal for external parties)
- Migration from legacy systems

**Reference selection criteria:**
1. Prioritize Norwegian public sector LMS references
2. Include at least one reference with 5,000+ user scale
3. Include at least one reference with complex integration landscape (SSO, SCIM, API)
4. Include references showing external user portal capability
5. Ensure reference contacts are available and briefed

### 8.3 Capacity Documentation (35% weight)

Must demonstrate:
- Sufficient technical staff for implementation (project manager, developers, integration specialists)
- Norwegian language capability in delivery team
- Financial capacity to sustain a 3-6 year contract
- Sub-contractor/partner capacity (if applicable, with Vedlegg 4)
